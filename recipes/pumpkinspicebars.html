Pumpkin Spice Bars

Description

PUMPKIN SEASON. Arguably, the best season of them all. The best flavor. Paired with cream cheese??? Are you kidding me?????

These pumpkin spice bars are to die for. But that's not part of the recipe, so live another day knowing you're going to make this and love life!

Ingredients

Cake Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin puree
  • 1 ⅔ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Frosting Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar

Directions

Making the Cake

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Beat pumpkin, sugar, oil, and eggs with an electric mixer in a medium bowl until well combined.
  4. Sift together flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl.
  5. Stir into pumpkin mixture until thoroughly combined.
  6. Spread batter evenly into an ungreased 10x15-inch jelly roll pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until cake bounces back when gently pressed, 25 to 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool.
  8. Make the frosting

Making the Frosting

  1. eat together butter and cream cheese until smooth; stir in vanilla.
  2. Add confectioners' sugar a little at a time, beating until mixture is smooth.
  3. Spread frosting evenly on top of cooled cake. Cut into 24 squares.
  4. Enjoy!
